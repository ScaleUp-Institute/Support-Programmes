How to read this dashboard

Programmes in each region are grouped into five themes. Many programmes span more than one theme, so duplicates across cards are intentional.

Leadership
Programmes that develop founders and senior teams to lead through growth phases. Includes structured leadership courses, executive coaching, cohort-based learning, peer networks and founder forums, mentoring and NED introductions, and governance/board development. Builds decision-making capability and reduces leadership isolation.
Talent and Skills
Support to attract, develop and retain people. Includes recruitment pipelines (graduates, apprentices, placements), upskilling and reskilling for technical and managerial roles, and applied training on operational skills (sales, marketing, finance, digital). Often delivered with universities, bootcamps or funded training providers.
Finance and Risk Capital
Access to capital across the funding journey. Includes equity (seed, growth, venture), debt and grants, tax reliefs (EIS/SEIS/R&D), introductions to investors, angel networks and lenders, plus investment-readiness support (pitch, model, due-diligence preparation).
Access to markets
Help reaching customers and securing sales. Covers domestic market development and export / international market entry (market selection, regulatory guidance, partner matching, trade missions), routes into corporate supply chains and large customers (meet-the-buyer events, pilots, supplier onboarding), and bid / procurement support for public and private buyers.
Infrastructure
Physical and digital infrastructure for scaling. Covers incubators, accelerators, science parks, co-working spaces, labs and specialist facilities, plus R&D and innovation support (university partnerships, prototyping, IP guidance, collaborative R&D funding). Value comes from community and proximity, not just space.
